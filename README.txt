CRASHRACER by pistillphil

!This game is a work in progress!

You drive through a desert with high velocity, because you flee! (Does not matter from what)
Avoid Obstacles!


Controls:
A:	Move Left
D:	Move Right 

M:	Mute/UnMute Music
